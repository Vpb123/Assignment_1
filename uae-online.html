UAE-Online

Residents of more than 180 countries can apply for a visa through the app. Nationals of more than 30 countries are eligibe for a free visa on arrival in Dubai, and do not need apply in advance.

How to apply and pay

Applications can be made a maximum of 58 days of minimum of 24 hours prioe to travel. Emirates and VFS recommend you apply as far in advance as possible.

Payment can be made with a visa/Master card credit or debit card only
